Heaven

This photo was taken in Kashgar, Xinjiang. If Heaven truly exist, it should be something like this. Some people have the stereotype that the south area of Xinjiang is dangerous. I think they are wrong. Kashgar should be added to the top 3 worth visiting places for its nature scenery and amazing culture.

Sky Like a Circle

It is the sky of my univeristy, Zhejiang University. I began to learn photography at this time. Just turn your camera to the sky and choose a low-speed exposure.

Village in Kanas' Arms

This photo was taken in Kanas, Xinjiang. Actually I planned to see the sunrise. However, the weather was not fine. But it is still wonderful take such pictures. That is not a pity.

Spring Train

This photo was taken in a rainy day in Hangzhou, China. I wanted to escape from the burden of my papers, so I went to Baita Park. Fortunate to find such a spot. After the rain, all the flowers dropped to ground and there was never a chance to see such scenery that year.
