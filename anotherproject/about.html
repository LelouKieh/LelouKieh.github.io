About Find by Painting

The gallery has collected XXX impressionism paintings. You can find impressionism painting(s) by Search View or Discover View.

Search view contains a table with a search bar. If you know the name of painting, use the search bar on right corner of the following table. This view is for those who have a specific aim or know the painting's name.

Discover view is a gallery view including all impressionism paintings. You can see their digital version and discover their names, details, exhibited in which museum, and more. This view is for those forget the painting's name or just want to discover impressionism paintings.

If you can not find the result, go to "Contact Me" and ask for help.

About Search View

Search View offers a search bar. Just type the name of impressionism paintings in it to see where they are!
